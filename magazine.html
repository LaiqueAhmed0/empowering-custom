Our Story

Slavica's first employment, after graduating with a Master degree in History of Communications, was at Hachette Filipacchi in Paris where she single handedly launched Femina. She helped design the layout, found the advertisers and, within 8 months, surpassed the sales objectives. The result was so noticeable that a team of 7 people came to take over. Femina is now one of the most distributed French magazines. She went back to Montreal where she worked in business development for numerous other publications, help create new concepts, marketing packages and tools, and new content/editorial to help drive sales. Year over year, she surpassed sales objectives. She went on to opening her own marketing and consulting company in 2005, wrote 44 books on success/mindset and 34 screenplays. She launched Empowering Entertainment in 2017 and is currently developing a slate of a dozen projects while also producing content for other filmmakers.
