Decks

Most filmmakers and entrepreneurs miss out on opportunities due to substantial lacks in their investors' deck

A Deck helps to position your genre (look book) and also show the potential cast, crew, investors and distributors that you are knowledgeable about the market place and how your will sell your film.

You need to show the cast that they will have a great visibility via your film and the investors, that they will get their money back and some.

Our basic decks are look book that include a synopsis, the comparable, potential cast, some minimum financial data.

We also tailor the deck to show the look and feel of the movie according to your requirements.
